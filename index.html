--- layout: post_notitle title: Knight-Mozilla OpenNews -- Building an ecosystem for open web development in journalism ---

OpenNews is a joint project of Mozilla and the Knight Foundation that supports the growing community of news developers, designers, and data reporters helping journalism thrive on the open web.

SRCCON heads to Portland, Oregon

We're back for another two days of group discussions and collaboration. (photo/Erik Westra)

This July, OpenNews' flagship annual conference SRCCON will be back for the third time. SRCCON is a hands-on conference focused on the practical challenges news technology and data teams encounter every day.

We work to make SRCCON an inclusive and welcoming event where people can feel comfortable digging into complex problems. We keep SRCCON intentionally small, which means tickets have already been allocated. But we're working on a bunch of options for remote participation and documenation. The SRCCON spirit will continue again at the Mozilla Festival in October.

About OpenNews

We believe a community of peers working, learning, and solving problems together can create the tools journalism needs to thrive. Through fellowships that place developers in news organizations around the world, conferences for newsroom designers and coders, and a wide range of community and project support programs, we support collaboration across organizations. Learn more about what we do or find out how you can get involved.
